Adaptive Pomodoro Clock

This is an experiment of an alternative "pomodoro" time tracking scheme, designed to avoid breaking your flow. It won't interrupt you with a break time when you complete one pomodoro (25 minutes), instead it will accumulate time for a longer break later.
